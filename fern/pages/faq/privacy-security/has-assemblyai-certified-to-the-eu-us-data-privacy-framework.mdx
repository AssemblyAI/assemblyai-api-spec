---
title: "Has AssemblyAI certified to the EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework?"
---

Yes, AssemblyAI has certified to the EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework (DPF) and adheres to the [DPF Principles](https://www.dataprivacyframework.gov/s/framework-text). You can view the AssemblyAI DPF certification [here](https://www.dataprivacyframework.gov/list). Please note that to locate the certification, search for "AssemblyAI" in the search bar.
