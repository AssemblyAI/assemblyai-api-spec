---
title: "How does AssemblyAI back up inputs to its system?"
---

Infrastructure components are deployed as code, services can be redeployed quickly using standard images. Transcripts are not backed up, but are sent to storage with 99.999999% durability (AWS S3). Clustered backup and backup regions are being prioritized by our team. For more information on how AssemblyAI maintains audio and transcript data, please see [here](https://support.assemblyai.com/articles/2240096256-does-assemblyai-offer-zero-data-retention).
