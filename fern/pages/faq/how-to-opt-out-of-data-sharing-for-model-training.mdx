# How to Opt Out of Data Sharing for Model Training

AssemblyAI provides an option for customers to opt out of having their data used for training our models. If you wish to opt out of this data sharing, follow these steps:

## Opting Out Process

1. Send an email to [data-opt-out@assemblyai.com](mailto:data-opt-out@assemblyai.com)
2. Ensure you send the email from the address associated with your AssemblyAI account
3. In the email, clearly state your request to opt out of data sharing for model training

## Important Notes

- This process is outlined in Section 4.4 of our [Terms of Service](https://www.assemblyai.com/legal/terms-of-service)
- Once we receive your opt-out request, we will process it accordingly
- If you have any questions about this process, you can contact our support team for assistance. To do that, email support@assemblyai.com

By following these steps, you can ensure that your data is not used for training our models while still benefiting from our services.
