AssemblyAI supports a wide range of languages across our speech-to-text models for pre-recorded audio. The available languages and features vary by model. Check out the [Models](/docs/getting-started/models) page to learn more about our different models and how to choose the best one for your use case.

When you submit a transcription request, our system automatically routes your audio to the best available model based on the detected or specified language and the models you provide in the `speech_models` parameter. For example, with `speech_models: ["universal-3-pro", "universal-2"]`, the system will use Universal-3-Pro for languages it supports (English, Spanish, Portuguese, French, German, and Italian) and automatically fall back to Universal-2 for all other languages. You can check which model processed your request using the `speech_model_used` field in the response. See the [Model selection](/docs/pre-recorded-audio/select-the-speech-model) page for more details.
